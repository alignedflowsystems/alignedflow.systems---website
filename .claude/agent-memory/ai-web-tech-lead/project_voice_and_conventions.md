---
name: Site voice and copy conventions
description: Copy rules for AlignedFlow Systems — brand-voice (no I/we/our/us), "Get a Quote" CTA, no avatar photos, realistic stats
type: project
---

The site uses brand-voice throughout all visible copy — avoid ALL first-person pronouns (both singular "I/me/my" and plural "we/us/our"). Updated in March 2026 after owner review.

Instead use:
- "AlignedFlow Systems" for the brand as actor ("AlignedFlow Systems handles...")
- "you/your" when addressing the client
- Passive or impersonal constructions ("Designs are shared...", "Every project includes...")
- Gerund or imperative forms ("Delivering websites...", "Get a site that...")

All primary CTA buttons across the site use the label "Get a Quote" — not "Book a Free Call", "Book a Call", or "Get In Touch" as a button label.

Testimonial cards do not display avatar photos — name and role text only.

Stats (in StatsCounter.tsx) are set to realistic solo-agency numbers: 3+ Years Experience, 12+ Happy Clients, 18+ Projects Delivered, 5★ Five-Star Reviews.

**Why:** Owner wants to avoid both solo-founder ("I") and agency-team ("we") impressions. Brand-voice is neutral and more professional. This was the owner's explicit decision in March 2026.

**How to apply:** Any new copy, components, or pages must avoid all first-person pronouns. Any new CTA buttons should use "Get a Quote" unless a specific different label is approved.
